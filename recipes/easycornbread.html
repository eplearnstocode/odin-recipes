Easy Cornbread

Easiest traditional cornbread!

This delightful side item is a tasty yet simple addition to any southern dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Martha While/White Lily butter milk corn meal mix (regular will do)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup buttermilk (regular milk to substitute)
  • 1/2 cup oil (canola or vegetable is best
  • 1 Tbsp butter to top muffins

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Add cornmeal to medium mixing bowl.
  3. Incorporate egg and buttermilk.
  4. Mix in oil.
  5. Grease muffin tin or spay with cooking spray. (This recipe can be cooked in regular pan or cast iron skillet if muffins not desired)
  6. Evenly fill muffin cups and put in oven.
  7. Bake for approximately 20 minutes, until toothpick inserted removes cleanly and tops are golden brown.
  8. Remove from oven add butter to tops. Once butter is melted tilt muffins to prevent them from turning soggy.
  9. Serve and enjoy.
