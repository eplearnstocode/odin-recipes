Air Fried Potatoes

A yummy but healthy way to get your carb fix!

This is a great side item to any meal where potatoes are welcome! A simple healthy substitute to french fries!

Ingredients

  • Air frier with basket
  • 6 medium to large potatoes (or desired amount)
  • Bottle of spray olive oil
  • Salt (garlic and other spices welcome)
  • Kraft Chipolte Aioli

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes and rinse.
  2. Slice to desired thickness. (if to thin they will be hard, if to thick they will burn before being done in the middle and you will need to adjust your temperature accordingly)
  3. Spray air frier basket.
  4. Evenly coat sliced potatoes in seasoning.
  5. Add potatoes to air frier basket and evenly coat with spray oil.
  6. Turn on air frier. For thicker potatoes start at 350 degrees for 10-15 minutes, then turn up to 390 degrees until desired texture and doneness. Thinner potatoes air fry on 390 for about 30 minutes.
  7. Gently stir /turn potatoes every 10 minutes. Take care to add oil and seasonings as needed.
  8. Serve with Chipolte Aioli or favorite dipping sauce.
