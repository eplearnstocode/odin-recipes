Clutch Punch

The perfect drink to clean out your liquor cabinet and chill!

This dangerously delicious beverage can be adapted to most forms of alcohol and to many mixers!

Ingredient

  • Sprite or other desired fizzy drink.
  • Clear liquor. (tequila, rum, vodka, gin)
  • Fruit for garnish
  • Lime juice or other fruit juice
  • Ice
  • Simple syrup, bitters, and/or flavored liqueurs

Directions

  1. Grab your favorite cup.
  2. Fill with ice.
  3. Add 2 shots of desired liquor.
  4. Add sprite or other fizzy drink.
  5. Add desired bitters or simple syrup.
  6. Garnish with fruit and fruit juice to taste.
  7. Enjoy responsibly!
